Why Use Us?

SUScube water tanks are manufactured from SUS304/2B food-grade stainless steel with a minimum nickel content of 8%, ensuring exceptional corrosion resistance, durability, and water purity. The 2B finish provides a smooth, easy-to-clean surface that prevents bacterial growth. Our advanced YW-150 high-frequency pulse welding and precision hydraulic stamping technologies guarantee consistent quality and structural integrity. Compared to traditional cement, iron, plastic or FRP tanks, SUScube tanks are lightweight, hygienic, easy to install, and offer superior cost performance — making them the ideal choice for modern water storage needs.
